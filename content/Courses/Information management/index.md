# Information Management for Researchers

**Organizing research materials, references, and resources systematically**

Learn to manage the information flow of academic work, from research papers to project materials.

## What You'll Learn

- Organize research literature and references
- Implement systematic filing systems
- Manage project materials effectively
- Use reference management tools
- Develop retrieval strategies

## Course Format

- **Duration:** 4-5 hours
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Format:** Self-paced with tools and templates
- **Status:** Published
