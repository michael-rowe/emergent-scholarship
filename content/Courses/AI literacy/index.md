# AI Literacy for Academic Practice

**Develop multidimensional capability with generative AI for academic work**

This course develops comprehensive AI literacy across six interconnected dimensions: access and recognition, critical evaluation, functional application, creation and communication, ethical awareness, and contextual judgement.

## Course structure

The course uses a developmental framework with four modules:

### Foundation module
Building conceptual understanding of AI as cognitive partner
- Lesson 1.1: AI as a cognitive partner
- Lesson 1.2: Prompting and communication

### Module 1: Substitution
Adding efficiency to existing practice
- Lesson 2.1: Creating content efficiently
- Lesson 2.2: Reading for information
- Lesson 2.3: Writing assistance

### Module 2: Adaptation
Reshaping practice around AI capabilities
- Lesson 3.1: Building competence
- Lesson 3.2: Argument development
- Lesson 3.3: Problem decomposition

### Module 3: Transformation
Structural integration into practice
- Lesson 4.1: Context sovereignty
- Lesson 4.2: Cultivating taste
- Lesson 4.3: Integrating into practice
