# Effective Note-Taking Systems

**Building personal knowledge systems for research and learning**

Develop systematic approaches to capturing, organizing, and retrieving knowledge across your academic work.

## What You'll Learn

- Choose and implement a note-taking system
- Develop effective capture and processing habits
- Link ideas for knowledge synthesis
- Build a personal knowledge base
- Maintain sustainable note-taking practices
